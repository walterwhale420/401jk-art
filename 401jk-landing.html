The Manifesto

Break the System

01

We Were Sold a Dream

Grind for decades, stash your earnings in a 401(k), and retire in peace. That was the deal. Every generation from Boomers to Zoomers was handed this same promise — and every generation watched it get quietly redefined. Retirement age goes up. Pension plans disappear. The finish line keeps moving.

02

Technology Should Free Us

Productivity has compounded for decades. Automation was supposed to mean more leisure, not more labor. Instead, governments responded by raising retirement ages — using the gains of technology to extend the working years of the people who built it. The math stopped making sense a long time ago.

03

We Built a Different Exit

Through blockchain, memes, and collective action, $401jK is a protest and a portfolio. We are not financial advisors. We are people who read the terms and conditions and decided to write our own. The system made retirement a joke. We made the joke into a token.
